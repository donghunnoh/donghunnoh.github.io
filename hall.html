'Hall' is a VR application designed to make the music experience immersive and fantastical using the virtual world.

Overview

Now than ever in history, we are connected through internet. The pros of this is anyone can have opportunity to access anything. Hall is a virtual concert mobile application that you can book and enjoy immersive concerts at home. The virtual concert that is affordable, accessible, and enjoyable.

Diverse concert experience from various artists.

Only about 3 steps, you will get your personal Home Screen. Tell us your favorite genre and artists. We will prepare you the magical experience.

Enjoy Interaction with other viewers in a intuitive way.

Imagine if you are in the concert hall. There is not going to be just you but full of people. Remember the sound of crowd. In the Hall, you can re-enjoy the moment of interaction that you had in physical concerts.

Experience in a different devices

Mobile application, VR application, and also PC version of Hall is now available. Enjoy experience in a different divices that is the easiest one for you.

Research Insights

Research insights always give me something unexpected. I could find that the genre doesn't matter for people who willing to join a virtual concert, and also inclusive option can be a really strong advantage of the product. Since virtual concert has no limitation, it could be the place that people can enjoy music regardless of location limitation or disabilities.
