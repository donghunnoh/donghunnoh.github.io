VITA is a new disaster preparation kit designed for people intimidated by unfamiliar survival kits. With the shape of the vest, we aim to be accessible, efficient, ergonomic. We believe that this kit applies to everyone and is great support from natural disasters.

My Role

1. Designed the holistic experience from the start to the end of the product.
2. Developed storyboards throughout the research.
3. Prototyped, Tested and Refined to make a final experience.
4. Created the consistent and intuitive look and feel across the Interface.

Accomplishment

- Usability testing average score: 73% positive results from initial designs.
- Created app design libraries and screens for apps and web.
- Designed physical product with a real measured capacity.

Design Process

Research -> Ideation -> Prototype (testing) -> Final refinement

Research

We took a various type of research method, which contains qualitative user interview, and quantitative research.

Research Shows: What kind of survival kit do people need to survive?

1. Practical and enough supplies.
2. Easy to use. Intuitive, one click product.
3. Affordable and Beautiful product that doesn't make people intimidated.

Final Solution

The problem that current products have is that they don’t have enough supplies for the real survival and kit is too heavy or very overpriced. In which, we seeked for blue ocean opportunity of having comparatively lower price, but at the same time having maximum practicality that supported by web and mobile applications.

Final Product: Before Disaster

Order your own kit

No more generic survival backpack. You can order Vest specifically for your region, what disaster you would like to prepare for. It will change the supplies, material of the vest, and even where we recommend locating the kit. Customize your kit by changing color and putting your medical information.

Place the vest wherever you want

Install your kit with the placement module that we provide with the kit. We provide step by step introduction through application. You can hang, roll, put anywhere your vest kit but we recommend just the place.

Manage your kit

Have you ever felt that it is hard to track your supplies? Now you don’t have to worry about taking care of expiration dates. VITA application will support you, and help you to easily order, receive, maintain your vest.

When Disaster Happens

Step-by-step instructions

In emergency situation, the application turns into a dark mode to safe energy. VITA vest has an emergency charger with batteries for mobile devices. System keep track of your location and send your location to your connected friends. It will keep update you what to do next.
