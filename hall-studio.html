Hall Studio is a virtual concert platform that allows any pianists to hold a concert with a music visualization based on playing.

Overview

Imagine you can hold a virtual concert that looks like for celebrities. Virtual concerts with limitless audiences, fully customized background, and the visualizer to emphasize your play.

Background

I was always curious about the fact that different pianists have a different nuance of music depending on their interpretations. It is the main reason people prefer different pianists, and also the reason people enjoy live concerts. So I wanted to figure out what makes a difference, and also if I can visualize the emotions.

Goals

1. Visualize music not just notes, but also interpretations.
2. Get inspired from prototyping method and make end result usable.
3. Make result accessible, easy, and reasonable.

Design Process

Creative Prototyping using Processing with ongoing research -> Interview with prototypes -> Make it to be useful (build application)

Creative Prototyping

To find out what elements are needed for visualizing music and emotions, I had gone through multiple prototyping process and try out different sensors and dataset.

Refinements, Prototypes,
And Finally Solution

After few rounds of refinement, I could find the most smooth experience while collecting decent amount of dataset for virtual concert. The solution came out after tying putting sensors to different garments, which came out to be headset that doesn't bother artists while track the movement of them.

Find a Way to Visualize Feeling

Key of the project was to find a way to map different physical information that we naturally interpret as part of the music and emotions.

Stretch sensors and gyroscope sensors will detect how fast musician breath (how intense they feel about music) and how much they move (how emotional and deep they express music) and the data became to be represented by color, position, and opacity of objects.

Next Step: Apply to be useful

Since the project started with creative prototyping and I found opportunities during parallel research and interviews, which people have desire making contents by their own and produce it to public with richer visuals.

So I decided to make entire experience under system which can be run in a tablet application.

How it Works: Finally 'Hall Studio'

Since the project started with creative prototyping and I found opportunities during parallel research and interviews, which people have desire making contents by their own and produce it to public with richer visuals.

So I decided to make entire experience under system which can be run in a tablet application.

If I had more time...

I am still passionate to develop this project. If I had more time, I would contact with A.I. specialists to discuss about mainly two things:

- Face recognition technology to update and generate music visualizer.
- The fidelity to generate background field for virtual concerts, and other detailed technologies within the experiences.
