I craft evocative experiences by blending multiple areas.

Hi, Nice to meet you! Growing up, I was one of those kids who was curious about everything, laying out multiple books in front of me, not able to decide on just one. What resonated was the combination of art and story which led me to my first degree in Industrial Design. Craving a closer connection to the user, I found my way to Interaction design.
‍
As a User Experience Designer, I craft evocative experiences that balance needs and wants in order to tell the story of desire. Staying open, I relate and empathize with others’ complex emotions, collecting insights through thoughtful investigation. Blending interaction, product, motion, and graphics, I design useful experiences that live in the hearts of the user.
