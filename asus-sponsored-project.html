Project Janus is a ASUS ROG laptop ecosystem for diverse workflows. Users will be able to swap out different modules for different needs with software support.

My Role

1. Designed the holistic experience from concept to the final product.
2. Defined user needs throughout user scenario based on research.
3. Created the consistent and intuitive look and feel across the Interface.
4. Produced end-to-end flows of experience.

Accomplishment

Our team pitched idea to a stakeholder and could get a very positive reaction. We designed one complete ecosystem that applies different situations for gamers even though the deadline was super tight.

Design Process

Project Kickoff -> Research -> Ideation -> Prototype -> Testing -> Final refinement

Problem Statement

Behind the scene, most of the gamers doesn't make money by playing games. This is a problem because their laptop should serve multiple purposes such as portable daily workflow, high computing power. How might we make an intuitive product with a simple solution but also allow users to use it for multiple usages in daily life?

Ideation

Based on the research that shows 52% of gamers consider playing game to fill time while taking a break, waiting or commuting, we found the desire of users want to optimize their setting for different usages.

Prototype & User Testing

Using cardboards, sketches and 3D printings, we made low fidelity prototype to test the best experience throughout the complex commuting situations. We tested 12 different users to get feedback, and we updated our final design based on it.

Target User & User Journey

Throughout the research and prototyping, we could specify a storyboard of the day in life of target user who aims to cover different purposes with a single product.

Final Solution

To support diverse setup that covers gaming, productivity, and mobility or stationary setup, Project Janus offer modular design contains tower, laptop and different computer modules.

Scenario 1.
Hardcore game at home

When user wants to play games that needs higher performances, user can easily add or swap out computer modules that has been designed under entire modular system. The Armoury Crate system will support user to disconnect modules easily as well.

Scenario 2.
Laptop on-the-go

When user needs to move to different places, it doesn't take a long time anymore. Armoury Crate application will support eject everything user would need such as SSD and graphic cards.

Scenario 3.
Gaming on-the-go

Now users don't need to carry multiple different gaming devices for on-the-go gaming. Game controller that the ecosystem would be the game changer for your mobile gaming experience. Just attach it to the screen of the laptop that can be disconnected with keyboard unit.

Scenario 4.
Power usage on-the-go

Don't worry even when you have to do power work on-the-go. Just attach the capsule that you have in your pouch. No more Remote Control or laggy performance.

Scenario 5.
Come back home

Coming back to home, when user put everything back together, it also will be a easy process for user. Just put screen and capsule parts back to the tower. It will now be ready for you to use your home desk setup.

If I had more time...
